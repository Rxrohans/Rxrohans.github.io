Low Back Pain Tracking App

Clinical App for Pain Tracking

Problem Statement

Clinicians and physiotherapists need a method to track patient-reported back pain across the day and correlate it with sleep, medication, and activity levels.

Objectives

  • Record pain levels in morning, afternoon, and evening
  • Link symptoms with lifestyle inputs (sleep, meds)
  • Deploy app for pilot testing at clinical setting

Solution

Built using Kotlin and integrated with Google AppScript & Sheets for cloud data collection. SQLite used for offline mode.

Skills Used

  • Kotlin
  • Google AppScript, SQLite
  • Android Studio
